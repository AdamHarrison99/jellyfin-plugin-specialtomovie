Deletion Behavior

When the TV episode is removed, its linked movie is also deleted via Jellyfin (including the hard link file, NFO, and any metadata Jellyfin manages).
Deleting either item removes both. Without this, only removing the TV episode removes the linked movie.
Warning: This will permanently delete your original media files. When a linked movie is deleted, the original episode and all its associated files (subtitles, images, NFO, trickplay data) will be permanently removed by Jellyfin. This cannot be undone.
